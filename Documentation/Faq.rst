        available options and defaults.

    ..  accordion-item:: Why are my default image/logo paths not the old ones?
        :name: image-logo-paths
        :header-level: 2

        Since version 1.0.3, bundled assets are referenced from
        ``Resources/Public/Pictures/`` and defaults in
        ``ext_conf_template.txt`` point to:

        - ``EXT:id_be_login/Resources/Public/Pictures/road.webp``
        - ``EXT:id_be_login/Resources/Public/Pictures/Ideative-logo.svg``
